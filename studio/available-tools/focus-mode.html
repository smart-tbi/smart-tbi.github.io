Focus Mode

hidden

Goal: Focus mode is a feature designed to eliminate the distraction of a cluttered Newsfeed while you are viewing your friends’ and groups’ Facebook posts.

TBI related Info: People with TBI often report challenges processing information when there is too much information on a webpage.

Feature: The focus mode feature grays out the background of your Facebook Newsfeed and highlights the post you want to read. This reduces visual complexity by allowing you to view one post at a time.
